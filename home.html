ABOUT

This May, 16th to 17th, BITS Pilani Dubai Campus with Kregzo as a Coorganizer is hosting their flagship 2- day festival - EmPower - the mustvisit event for students, entrepreneurs, MNCs, Academia around the fields of business, entrepreneurship, finance and technology. A festival consisting of real-life simulations of the Stock market, Startup Pitch Battles, Business Seminars & Panels plus much more. This event is a one-of-its-kind that brings together bright minds from across industries to network & get immersed in innovation & the future of business. BITS Pilani Dubai Campus is creating a space where students from all across UAE will get to present their trading skills, business skills, marketing strategies, and possibly get an investor for their startup ideas and potential business ventures. We are expecting students from 20+ universities across UAE. Moreover, EmPower is designed to create the much-needed bridge between the future innovators of the UAE & the present mega minds. Come, and witness as we, BITS Pilani & Kregzo create the first wave of Student-led Entrepreneurship history with EmPower.

VENUE

EmPower will be held on the grounds of BITS Pilani Dubai Campus located at the heart of Academic City in Dubai which is a prominent hub for University students across the MENA region including national and international students.

EVENTS

DAY 1

PITCH VERSE

University Startups from all over UAE Pitch their Ideas and Compete on a stage for investment and a three tier prizes. This event will take place in two rounds. Round 1: Elevator Pitch Round Round 2: This will be a 5-minute pitch event for all the selected participants, followed by 3 minute Q & A with a esteemed panel of judges/investors, followed by a networking session with judges, VIPs and sponsors.

NETWORKING EVENT

Pitching Competition of EmPower, PitchVerse will be followed by a networking night where students will interact with mentors from the industry of business and finance.

START UP EXHIBITION

An area will be dedicated to the registered start-ups to exhibit their ideas and inventions. Potential investors, esteemed guests and the audience can interact with the start-up ventures after the opening ceremony. This allows the students from universities across UAE to come together and showcase their extraordinary ideas on this platform.

PANEL MEET

This Interactive Panel will hold professionals from government, corporate, and academic sectors. It will serve as a platform to introduce the young minds to an amalgamation of perspectives from these three sectors.

MOCK STOCK

A trading event in which participants can invest money in a demo account. A fixed amount will be given to everyone on an online platform The one who makes the most money wins.

MONEY HEIST

You are stuck in the royal mint of Spain, with bullets firing all around. This time your wildest imaginations are coming true. You have to use the power of your brain to accomplish the mission. Beat different leaves, dealing with a variety of situations to lead your team to a successful heist. The competing teams stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes.

CEO CLUB

Welcome to the Real-life battle of Mergers and Acquisitions. Participating teams can build their companies from the ground up. Post this, the floor will be open for negotiations, mergers, and acquisitions amongst companies. The strategies will be judged by our mentors and the company with the best approach, strategy, ability to think on its feet and net worth will be chosen as the winner.

GREEN BUSINESS BUILDING

A University-based Sustainability Challenge which will be presented by the organizing team prior to the event. The participants then need to present a solution on the event day. The ideas will be judged based on their merit. Post-event proposal of the solution will be taken forward with the respective authorities for further implementation in the Universities. Additionally, a cash prize will be awarded to the winning team.

DAY 2

HIGHER EDUCATION FAIR

Students will be provided with guidance on pursuing their higher education in the fields of economics, business, management, etc. Various consultancies and relevant companies will be invited to set up their booths within the campus.

FACULTY PITCH COMPETITION

To make the festival more inclusive, we will conduct a faculty pitch competition where the teachers from various universities including BITS can pitch their ideas to potential investors. Directors of the respective Universities will be invited to sit alongside the judges. This allows the Directors to know the kind of substantial research projects or ideas taking place within the university and can thus allocate money/resources for a particular research project i.e., the winner.

FUTURE READINESS SEMINAR

A seminar on Blockchain Technology, its security features, and the various domains it is used in (Cryptocurrencies, NFTs and Ethereum). Participants will get hands-on experience in creating their own Crypto coin, creating NFT art, selling it, and creating smart Contracts. It will be accompanied by a mock crypto trading event which shall have a live leaderboard and exciting prizes for the winners.

IPL AUCTION

This will be an auction simulation game where the participants will be given a list of players, their qualifications, their abilities, and their pricing. Each participant will start with the initial spending allocated to them. This game comes with a twist wherein we will be giving out additional information about players through the course of the game.

REAL-LIFE MONOPOLY

Monopoly is the classic fast-dealing property trading board game. This will take place as a lifesized shortened version of the original game. Rules of the original Monopoly will apply. Students from various colleges can participate either individually or in teams to win exciting prizes.

ADZAP

This event persuades the students to instantaneously decide on a skit that would depict an ad for any product that is given to them. The ad can be in the form of role-playing. Participants can make use of props. The duration of the ad is variable. Being able to advertise the given product with wit can help you win this one!

TALK INTERACTIVE

We will host prominent figures from the industry of business and finance who have been through the start-up journey to share their experience. The event will be moderated by Kregzo. Proposed guests include Social Media Influencers from the following industries: 1.Fashion 2. Food 3. Women in Tech/Business to inspire the younger generation.
